Lemon Pancakes

>

Ingredients

  • 200g self raising flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon gold/brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 200ml milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 25g melted butter
  • 1 lemon, zest
  • lemon curd

Method

Step 1

Whisk all of the pancake ingredients together in a bowl until it's all nicely blended. Then, heat the butter in a frying pan on low, or medium-low, heat until foaming.

Step 2

Drop 2 teaspoons of the mixture and shape it with a spoon to form a 8-9cm disc. Cook for 2-3 min and then flip, and cook an extra minute.

*Optional* If your pan is too small to cook multiples at once, you can keep cooked ones in the oven at the lowest setting to keep warm.

Step 3

Stack the pancakes, optionally with some lemon juice in between, and drizzle with lemon curd, scattering zest on top.


The final result will be a juicy, lemon flavoured pancake, not for the citrus averse.
