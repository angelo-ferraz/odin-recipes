Lemon Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 4tsp olive oil
  • 500g sliced leeks
  • 4 thyme sprigs w/ leaves picked
  • 2 x 400g cans butter beans
  • 500ml vegetable bouillon
  • 2 tsp wholegrain mustard
  • small flat leaf parsley
  • 40g chopped kale, no tough stems
  • 25g chopped hazelnuts
  • Dried tomato or bacon, optional

Method

Step 1

Heat 1tsp oil over low heat, adding the leeks, thyme and seasoning. Cover and cook for 15min until soft, adding a bit of water if the leeks stick.

Step 2

Add butter beans and the water from the can, the stock, and mustard, bringing to boil and letting simmer for 3-4min.

Step 3

Blend soup in a processor, or blender, stirring the parsley and seasoning.

Step 4

In another pan, add remaining oil as well as the kale and hazelnuts. Stir until hazelnuts are toasted and kale crisps


Serve, and sprinkle the kale-hazelnut mixture, with optional tomato/bacon, onto the soup, adding water if the soup has thickened.
